Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FinSim

Is FinSim a real bank?

No, FinSim is a banking simulator designed for educational purposes and portfolio demonstration. All transactions and accounts are simulated.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your data is stored locally and never shared with third parties.

Can I use this for real banking?

FinSim is for simulation and educational purposes only. For real banking, please use licensed financial institutions.

Is there a mobile app?

FinSim is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on all devices. You can install it on your home screen for app-like experience.
